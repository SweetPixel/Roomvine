HOW IT WORKS



Roomvine is currently free for all selected Beta users. You just need to follow 3 simple steps


Claim your venue on Roomvine by filling out the form below. Verification can take from 1-7 days.


Get your customers to interact on Roomvine by placing this sticker or sign at the entrance. You can download the sign from here.


Download the app and sign in the with the email address which you have submitted in the venue claim form.

You're all set! Check-in to your venue on a device present there. Get insights in a more fun and real way.
